I specialize in designing scalable architectures, leading product development, and building agentic AI frameworks. I am passionate about maintaining data control through self-hosted solutions and integrating state-of-the-art LLMs into modern web applications. 

* 🔭 **Currently building:** **Jestr** (a scalable video consultation and talent marketplace) and **OpenClaw** (an agentic automation framework).
* 🏢 **Founder:** **The Managers Playbook**, sharing insights on technical leadership and product strategy.
* 💡 **Current focus:** Architecting full-stack solutions, deploying local Large Language Models (LLMs), and crafting complex workflows with self-hosted automation tools.
* 🤝 **Open to collaborate on:** Open-source AI tools, self-hosted infrastructure projects, and scalable SaaS platforms.
* 💬 **Ask me about:** System architecture, Next.js, Laravel, LLM integrations, or local AI environments.
